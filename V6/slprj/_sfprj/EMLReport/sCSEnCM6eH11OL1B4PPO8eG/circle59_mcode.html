167 function circle(x,y,r)
168%x and y are the coordinates of the center of the circle
169%r is the radius of the circle
170%0.01 is the angle step, bigger values will draw the circle faster but
171%you might notice imperfections (not very smooth)
172ang=0:0.01:2*pi; 
173xp=r*cos(ang);
174yp=r*sin(ang);
175plot(x+xp,y+yp);
176hold on
